Privacy Policy

In this Privacy Policy, “Personal Data” generally refers to any information that directly or indirectly relates to a living person who can be identifiable.

Purpose of Use of Personal Data

We may use your personal data for the following purposes:

• Establish and manage your Yacai Finance account;

• Contact you and respond to your inquiry;

• Enable you to use our free services to seek product information;

• Enable you to fully use all of our free services on the APP;

• Enable you to submit a product purchase application to a cooperative bank via OpenAPI, or go to the bank’s APP where you can complete online application form;

• Enable us to deal with your application for buying cooperative banking products on your behalf (for details, please refer to the “Cooperative Bank Products” section below);

• Analyze customer behavior, market trends and customer base characteristics so that we can improve our service, enhance our communication with you and all other customers and provide services that better suit your needs;

• Allow the APP to save your personal data so that you do not need to re-enter the relevant information each time when you apply for a product;

• Enable us to save the data more effectively and manage our operations;

• Use your personal data for direct promotion purposes under the premise of your consent in advance (see the Direct Promotions section for details);

• Comply with legal and regulatory requirements;

• Enable us to provide services and products for the reasons described above and/or for your use of the APP.

Cooperative Bank Products

We can help you save a great deal of time when you apply for the bank products and services (hereinafter referred to as “cooperative bank products”).
